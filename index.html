Hygiene & Quality Standards

Your safety is our top priority

HACCP Certified

Our facility follows Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points standards to ensure food safety at every step of production. HACCP is a management system in which food safety is addressed through the analysis and control of biological, chemical, and physical hazards from raw material.

ISO 22000 Compliant

We maintain international food safety management system standards for consistent quality and safety. ISO's food safety management standards help organizations identify and control food safety hazards, at the same time as working together with other ISO

Sterile Environment

Our production areas are maintained with hospital-grade cleanliness and regular sanitization protocols. Maintaining personal hygiene means coming into the bakery with clean clothes and wearing a hair net or hat to keep your hair out of the way.
